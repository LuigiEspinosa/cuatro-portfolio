## My Journey as a Web Developer: A Transformational First Job Experience

Entering the tech industry as a Web Developer can be both thrilling and overwhelming, and for me, it all began at [Namecol](https://namecol.com/) in February 2017. As I stepped into the world of web development in Bogotá D.C., Colombia, little did I know that this experience would shape my career and lay the foundation for a successful journey in the tech realm. In this blog post, I'll recount my first job experience, the challenges I faced, the skills I honed, and how it set me on the path to becoming a proficient Web Developer.

![Namecol Banner](/blog/working-at/blog-1.png)

## Embracing the Web

During my time at Namecol Internet, one of the crucial aspects I tackled was integrating responsive design techniques into websites. In a rapidly evolving digital landscape, mobile traffic was on the rise, and catering to mobile users became imperative. By implementing responsive design practices, I enabled websites to adapt seamlessly to various devices.

As a Web Developer, I soon realized that simply building visually appealing websites was not enough; they needed to be discoverable by search engines. To address this, I delved into the world of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) strategies. Through meticulous keyword research, optimized meta tags, and streamlined website structure.

## Technologies that Paved the Way:

Throughout my first job experience, I had the opportunity to work with a diverse set of technologies, including HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript/jQuery, PHP/Symfony, PrestaShop. Also, collaborate on the development of the internal CMS of the company. Each of these tools played a pivotal role in shaping my understanding of web development. As I expanded my knowledge, I found myself exploring innovative ways to leverage these technologies to create efficient and visually appealing websites.

---

Looking back at my journey from a newcomer to an established Web Developer, my time at Namecol Internet stands out as a transformative period. It taught me the importance of adaptability, collaboration, and continuous learning in the tech industry. Integrating responsive design, mastering SEO, and working with various technologies broadened my skill set and opened doors to numerous opportunities. Aspiring developers embarking on their own tech journeys should embrace every experience, no matter how small, as each step contributes to personal and professional growth. With passion, dedication, and a thirst for knowledge, one can thrive and make a significant impact in the ever-evolving world of web development.
